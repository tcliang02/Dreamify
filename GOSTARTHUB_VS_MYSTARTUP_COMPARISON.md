# Go Start Hub vs MyStartup: Key Differences

## Executive Summary

While both **Go Start Hub** and **MyStartup** support Malaysia's startup ecosystem, they serve different stages, audiences, and purposes. They are **complementary** rather than competitive, creating a complete innovation pipeline when working together.

---

## 1. FUNDAMENTAL DIFFERENCES

| Aspect | Go Start Hub | MyStartup |
|--------|--------------|-----------|
| **Type** | Private/Independent Platform | Government-Backed Initiative |
| **Launched By** | Private organization | Ministry of Science, Technology & Innovation (MOSTI) |
| **Primary Focus** | Student Innovation | Startup Ecosystem |
| **Target Audience** | Students & Recent Graduates | All Startup Stages |
| **Entry Point** | Idea/Prototype Stage | Startup Stage |
| **Scope** | Student-focused, specialized | Comprehensive, ecosystem-wide |

## 5. FUNDING OPPORTUNITIES

### Go Start Hub
- **Role:** Funding Discovery & Aggregation
- **Sources:** Multiple (businesses, investors, grants)
- **Amounts:** RM20K - RM300K+
- **Focus:** Student-friendly funding
